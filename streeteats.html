StreetEats Design Process


Description

StreetEats is a food rating app that locates cheap, authentic street food vendors near a user. It is a playful and colorful app that allows users to post pictures and leave ratings of street vendors. The app is targeted mostly towards tourists who want to find delicious food options that are popular with locals. It is available on smartphones, which is useful for tourists who are on-the-go.

Eat Sweet and Travel Cheap with StreetEats

This app allows the user to see a list and a map of street food options around them, which they can filter by the type of food (meal, snack, drink, dessert). Each street vendor has its own page, which contains the description, ratings, pictures, and the location of the vendor.
