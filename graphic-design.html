GRAPHIC DESIGN

Fly SFO

I used flat shapes and minimal colors to design an imaginary flyer for the San Francisco International Airport. I overlapped airplanes, the airport, the skyline, and the city landmark (the Golden Gate Bridge) to create a feeling of depth.

Yearbook Application Deadline

This is a set of Facebook flyers released across four days alerting students to apply for my school's yearbook team. The designs behind 'Y', 'B', and 'K' reflect the yearbook covers from the previous three years.

Pup-toberfest

This is a T-shirt design I made for Delaware Pet Rescue's annual event, the Pup-toberfest.

T4

Senior Superlatives

This is a Facebook flyer notifying seniors in high school to submit votes for senior superlatives (Best Dressed, Future Olympian, etc.).
