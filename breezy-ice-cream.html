Breezy Ice Cream Design Process


Ideation

Description: Breezy Ice Cream is an ice cream vending machine that dispenses delicious and fresh ice cream at the click of a button. The user can customize their order using a seamless, fun interface that allows them to choose the size, flavor, and toppings for their ice cream. Breezy Ice Cream automates the experience of going to an ice cream shop and allows people to get ice cream anywhere, from malls to arcades to playplaces.

Functions:

  • Allow users to customize their ice cream order
    • Size
    • Flavor
    • Toppings
  • Direct users to make their payment with card or cash
  • Notify users and owner of vending machine when an ice cream flavor is sold out

Audience:

Roles:

  • Young children
  • Teenagers
  • Parents of young children

Demographics:

  • Gender: all genders
  • Age: 3-30
  • Education: primary and secondary school
  • Income: limited income
  • Location: indoor recreational areas (ex. malls, arcades, playplaces, recreational centers), suburban

Psychographics:

  • Personality & Attitudes: energetic, social, na√Øve, curious
  • Values: open-minded, value spending time with family and friends
  • Lifestyles:
    • Children: attend school and have free time on weekends
    • Teens: attend school and work part-time jobs
    • Parents: work an office job and have family time on weekends
