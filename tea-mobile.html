Tea Mobile Design Process


Ideation

Description: Tea Mobile is a delivery-only boba store delivering affordable and customizable drinks to college students around Midtown Atlanta.

Elevator Pitch: Need a quick treat for yourself or catering for a group event? Tea Mobile delivers refreshing, authentic, and affordable boba right to you, perfect for study sessions, movie nights, and small events.

Audience: College students and event organizers for college events

User Profile:

Michelle (the studious student):

  • 23-year-old Georgia Tech student
  • Pursuing a PhD in Digital Media
  • Studies alone for hours in the library
  • Has a small circle of friends she hangs out with on weekends
  • Has only tried boba once or twice before
  • "No pain no gain"

Brandon (the party student):

  • 18-year-old Georgia Tech student
  • Pursuing a Bachelor’s in Mechanical Engineering
  • Parties every weekend
  • Just moved from Vancouver, a place with lots of boba shops
  • Drinks boba every other day and keeps a stock in his fridge
  • “I don’t study much but I get good grades”

Mary (housing department staff member):

  • Building/hall director for a small freshman dorm at Georgia Tech
  • Has a budget to organize dorm events
  • Has never tried boba before
  • Loving mother of three kids
  • Lives close to campus
  • "I want to make freshman year a good experience for everyone”

Strategy:

User Needs:

  • Find out if the restaurant delivers to their area
  • Order food online
  • Customize their order
  • Change the quantity of drinks they ordered
  • Track delivery
  • Contact the restaurant
  • View the restaurant menu
  • Learn more about boba

Client Needs:

  • To sell food online that will be delivered
  • Provide a system for order customization
  • Show their colorful, fun brand identity
  • Communicate reliability
  • Communicate quality
  • Promote new or popular items on front page
  • Encourage users to buy more items
