UI/UX DESIGN

Tea Mobile

Tea Mobile is a boba delivery website designed for college students. Its vibrant but straightforward design makes it easy for users to order drinks quickly and for the business to promote their teas.

Prototype Design Process

Breezy Ice Cream

Breezy Ice Cream is an ice cream vending machine that dispenses fresh ice cream at the click of a button. I prototyped what the ordering screen would look like, adding playful graphics and animations that would appeal to its young audience.

Prototype Design Process

Oversubscribed

Oversubscribed is an app that helps everyday users keep track of their free trials and subscriptions.

Prototype Design Process

StreetEats

StreetEats is a food rating app that locates cheap, authentic street food vendors near a user. It has a playful design and allows users to rate and post pictures of street vendors.

This customization screen of a ice cream vending machine allows customers to choose various toppings that they want on their ice cream. When customers select certain toppings, they show up on the ice cream illustration on the top.

Breezy Ice Cream - Home

800 px × 1200 px, Adobe XD

This is the home screen of a ice cream vending machine that allows customers to customize their ice cream order. The columns of ice cream illustrations in the background are animated (see prototype) and capture the eye of customers that walk by the vending machine.
